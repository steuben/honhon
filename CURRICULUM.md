# HonHon - Complete A1 French Curriculum

**12-Lesson Progressive Course for German Speakers**

## Curriculum Overview

This curriculum takes absolute beginners from zero French to A1 competency (CEFR) through 12 structured lessons. Each lesson builds systematically on previous content while maintaining immediate practical utility.

**Total Duration**: ~15-20 hours of study
**Target Proficiency**: A1 (Basic User)
**Language Pair**: German → French

---

## Lesson Structure

### LEÇON 1: Premiers Pas (First Steps)
**Duration**: 90-120 minutes
**Learning Objectives**:
- Greet people formally and informally
- Introduce yourself (name, origin)
- Use basic courtesy phrases
- Understand French pronunciation fundamentals

**Grammar**: Subject pronouns (je, tu, vous), Verb être (je suis, tu es, vous êtes)
**Vocabulary**: Greetings, courtesy phrases, countries/nationalities (40+ items)
**Prerequisites**: None
**Outcomes**: Can introduce yourself and respond to greetings

---

### LEÇON 2: Chiffres et Informations Personnelles
**Duration**: 90-120 minutes
**Learning Objectives**:
- Count from 0-30
- Give phone number and age
- Ask simple yes/no questions
- Spell your name in French

**Grammar**: Question formation (intonation), Verb avoir (j'ai, tu as, vous avez), Articles un/une
**Vocabulary**: Numbers 0-30, personal information, French alphabet
**Prerequisites**: Lesson 1 (être conjugation, greetings)
**Outcomes**: Can exchange basic personal information

---

### LEÇON 3: La Famille et les Relations
**Duration**: 90-120 minutes
**Grammar**: Possessive adjectives (mon/ma/mes, ton/ta/tes), Plural formation, Il y a
**Vocabulary**: Family members, relationships, quantity
**Prerequisites**: Lessons 1-2
**Outcomes**: Can describe family structure

---

### LEÇON 4: Professions et Activités
**Duration**: 90-120 minutes
**Grammar**: Present tense -ER verbs (travailler, parler, habiter), Question words (Qu'est-ce que)
**Vocabulary**: Professions, common activities, languages
**Prerequisites**: Lessons 1-3
**Outcomes**: Can state profession and daily activities

---

### LEÇON 5: La Maison et les Lieux
**Duration**: 90-120 minutes
**Grammar**: Definite articles (le/la/les), Prepositions of place, Negation (ne...pas), Verb aller (introduction)
**Vocabulary**: Rooms, furniture, places in city, locations
**Prerequisites**: Lessons 1-4
**Outcomes**: Can describe home and express location

---

### LEÇON 6: Le Temps et les Activités Quotidiennes
**Duration**: 90-120 minutes
**Grammar**: Time expressions (Il est...), Reflexive verbs (se lever, se coucher), Days of week
**Vocabulary**: Time, days, daily activities, frequency adverbs
**Prerequisites**: Lessons 1-5
**Outcomes**: Can tell time and describe daily routine

---

### LEÇON 7: La Nourriture et les Courses
**Duration**: 90-120 minutes
**Grammar**: Partitive articles (du/de la/des), Verb vouloir, Verb prendre, Quantity expressions
**Vocabulary**: Food, beverages, meals, restaurant phrases
**Prerequisites**: Lessons 1-6
**Outcomes**: Can order food and shop for groceries

---

### LEÇON 8: Les Vêtements et les Couleurs
**Duration**: 90-120 minutes
**Grammar**: Demonstrative adjectives (ce/cette/ces), Adjective agreement, C'est vs Il est
**Vocabulary**: Clothing, colors, sizes, descriptive adjectives
**Prerequisites**: Lessons 1-7
**Outcomes**: Can describe clothing and express shopping preferences

---

### LEÇON 9: La Météo et les Saisons
**Duration**: 90-120 minutes
**Grammar**: Impersonal il (Il fait, Il pleut), Verb faire, Preposition en, Temperature expressions
**Vocabulary**: Weather, seasons, months, feelings
**Prerequisites**: Lessons 1-8
**Outcomes**: Can describe weather and express sensations

---

### LEÇON 10: Les Transports et les Directions
**Duration**: 90-120 minutes
**Grammar**: Imperative mood (tournez, allez), Verb prendre (with transport), Direction prepositions
**Vocabulary**: Transportation, directions, places, movement verbs
**Prerequisites**: Lessons 1-9
**Outcomes**: Can ask for and give directions

---

### LEÇON 11: Les Loisirs et les Hobbies
**Duration**: 90-120 minutes
**Grammar**: Faire + de + article, Verb jouer + à/de, Frequency adverbs, Aimer + infinitive
**Vocabulary**: Activities, sports, instruments, entertainment
**Prerequisites**: Lessons 1-10
**Outcomes**: Can talk about hobbies and interests

---

### LEÇON 12: Plans et Projets
**Duration**: 90-120 minutes
**Grammar**: Futur proche (aller + infinitive), Verb pouvoir, Conditional politeness
**Vocabulary**: Time expressions, invitations, acceptance/refusal, activities
**Prerequisites**: Lessons 1-11
**Outcomes**: Can make plans and invitations

---

## Curriculum Progression Logic

### Phase 1: Foundation (Lessons 1-3)
Core identity, greetings, personal information. Establishes être/avoir mastery.

### Phase 2: Daily Life Core (Lessons 4-6)
Regular verb patterns, survival topics (work, home, time), essential interactions.

### Phase 3: Practical Transactions (Lessons 7-8)
Transactional competence (ordering, shopping), partitive articles, adjective agreement.

### Phase 4: Environmental Context (Lessons 9-10)
Physical world situational language (weather, directions), impersonal structures, imperative.

### Phase 5: Social Integration (Lessons 11-12)
Social participation (hobbies, invitations), near future, completing A1 competency.

## German Speaker Advantages

1. **Grammatical gender** concept already understood
2. **Formal/informal register** (tu/vous ≈ du/Sie)
3. **Compound tenses** background for future learning
4. **Extensive cognates** especially in cultural/academic vocabulary

## German→French Challenges Addressed

1. **Nasal vowels** - Progressive introduction from Lesson 1
2. **Silent letters** - Practiced throughout with spelling
3. **Partitive articles** - Dedicated attention in Lesson 7
4. **Adjective placement** - After noun vs. German before
5. **Pronunciation fluidity** - Liaison and elision gradually introduced

## A1 Competency Achievement

By curriculum completion, learners can:
- ✅ Introduce themselves and exchange personal information
- ✅ Ask and answer simple questions about familiar topics
- ✅ Understand basic spoken French in predictable situations
- ✅ Read simple texts (signs, menus, short messages)
- ✅ Write short notes and fill out forms
- ✅ Navigate French-speaking environments for basic needs
- ✅ Handle simple transactional exchanges
- ✅ Describe people, places, and daily routines simply
